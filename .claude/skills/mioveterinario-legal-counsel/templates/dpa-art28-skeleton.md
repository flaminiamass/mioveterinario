# Data Processing Agreement skeleton - platform as processor for vet/clinic

Use when MioVeterinario processes personal data on behalf of a vet/clinic, e.g. hosting agenda, patient/referto records, or documents strictly on vet instructions.

## 1. Parties

Controller: [Vet/Clinic legal name]
Processor: [MioVeterinario legal name]

## 2. Subject matter

Processor provides software services for [agenda/referti/patient records/document sharing] as described in [agreement/order form].

## 3. Duration

From [date] until termination plus deletion/return period.

## 4. Nature and purpose

- Hosting and making available data to controller and authorized users.
- Sending notifications if instructed.
- Technical maintenance, security, backup, support.
- No use for processor's own purposes except security, service operation, legal compliance, and as otherwise agreed.

## 5. Data subjects and categories

Data subjects:

- pet owners;
- vets/clinic staff;
- platform staff where relevant.

Data categories:

- account/contact data;
- pet data;
- booking data;
- confidential veterinary records/referti;
- invoice/payment references where included;
- logs/security data.

## 6. Controller instructions

Processor acts only on documented instructions of controller unless required by law. If an instruction appears unlawful, processor informs controller.

## 7. Confidentiality

Processor ensures authorized persons are bound by confidentiality and access is limited to need-to-know.

## 8. Security measures

Attach technical and organizational measures:

- TLS;
- encryption at rest/KMS;
- RBAC;
- MFA for admin/vets;
- audit logs;
- backups;
- incident response;
- logging redaction;
- vulnerability management;
- access review.

## 9. Subprocessors

[List subprocessors or link.] Processor may appoint subprocessors only under equivalent obligations and with notice/objection process.

## 10. Assistance

Processor assists controller with:

- data subject requests;
- DPIA;
- security incidents and breach notifications;
- audits and information requests;
- deletion/export.

## 11. Breach notification

Processor notifies controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach, with available details and updates.

## 12. Return/deletion

At termination, processor deletes or returns data according to controller instruction, unless law requires retention. Backups are deleted on normal rotation.

## 13. Audits

Processor makes information available and allows reasonable audits under agreed confidentiality and security limits.
