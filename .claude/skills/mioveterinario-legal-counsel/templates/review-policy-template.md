# Review policy template - MioVeterinario

## 1. Who can write a review

A review can be published only by an owner who [booked and completed] a visit with the veterinarian through MioVeterinario. If we describe a review as "verified", it means [technical verification method].

## 2. What is allowed

Reviews should describe the user's real experience with booking, communication, clinic organization, and the veterinary service, in a respectful and truthful way.

## 3. What is not allowed

Do not publish:

- false or manipulated reviews;
- reviews paid or incentivized without disclosure;
- insults, threats, harassment, discrimination;
- defamatory accusations or unverifiable criminal allegations;
- confidential veterinary, medical, fiscal, or third-party personal data;
- full names/contact data of third parties;
- spam, advertising, or irrelevant content;
- content that violates law or professional rules.

## 4. Moderation

MioVeterinario may reject, hide, edit only for redaction, or remove reviews that violate this policy or law. We may ask for evidence that the review is based on a real appointment. We keep moderation logs.

## 5. Vet replies

Veterinarians may reply professionally and respectfully. Vet replies must not reveal confidential clinical details, owner data, or third-party data.

## 6. Reporting and complaints

Users and vets can report a review at [link/email]. We will review the report and communicate the result where required. If content is restricted, we may provide reasons and complaint/appeal options according to applicable law.

## 7. Ranking and display

Reviews may influence profile visibility and search ranking. Main factors: [rating average, number of reviews, recency, reliability, relevance]. Sponsored placement, if any, will be labelled.
