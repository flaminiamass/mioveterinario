# Vet verification policy template

## Verification states

- draft: profile created, not public.
- pending_verification: documents submitted.
- verified: public and bookable.
- limited: public but not bookable or limited features.
- suspended: hidden or not bookable due to risk.
- terminated: account closed.

## Data to collect

- Full legal name.
- Professional title.
- Albo/order registration details.
- Clinic/structure legal name and address.
- VAT/tax identifiers.
- Representative authorization if clinic account.
- Contact details.
- Professional insurance details if required by contract.
- Specializations/titles evidence where claimed.

## Checks

- Identity consistency.
- Albo/order status.
- Clinic authorization.
- Sanitary structure/license where applicable.
- Specialization/title accuracy.
- Sanctions/suspension red flags if available and lawfully checked.

## Product gates

- No booking before verified.
- No prescription/referto before verified.
- No "verified profile" badge without completed check.
- Sponsored placement only for verified vets.
- Re-check every [period] or after complaints.

## Evidence retention

Retain verification evidence for [period] after account termination or dispute, subject to legal/privacy review.

## Suspension triggers

- False professional information.
- Expired/suspended registration.
- Serious user complaint involving safety/fraud.
- Illegal or misleading content.
- Security compromise.
- Non-payment/contract breach.
