### 2. What data we process

Depending on how you use MioVeterinario, we may process:

- account and contact data: name, email, phone, login data;
- pet data: pet name, species, breed, date of birth, weight, sex, microchip and similar information;
- booking data: selected vet, date/time, visit type, status, cancellation, notes;
- confidential veterinary data: referti, diagnosis, treatments, drugs, advice, vaccine records, if entered by a veterinarian or uploaded by you;
- location/address data: city, clinic address, home-visit address, approximate or precise location if enabled;
- invoice and payment data: invoice items, amounts, payment status, PSP transaction identifiers;
- reviews and content: ratings, comments, vet replies, moderation reports;
- technical/security data: IP address, device/browser data, logs, cookies, fraud/security events;
- marketing data: consent choices, email preferences, campaign source.

Do not enter human health data or third-party personal data in notes unless it is strictly necessary for the requested service.
