Remote consultations/video tools do not replace an in-person veterinary visit when the veterinarian considers it necessary. The veterinarian may refuse or interrupt a remote consultation and ask for an in-person visit.

## 8. Vet responsibilities

- comply with professional, deontological, tax, consumer, privacy, and prescription rules;

- keep credentials secure;
- not publish misleading advertising or fake reviews.
